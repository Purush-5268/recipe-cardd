🍫 Chocolate Cake

⏱ Prep: 45 mins 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Serves: 4
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Time Left: 45:00

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir well.
  4. Pour into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
